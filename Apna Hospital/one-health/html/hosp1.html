Medica Superspecialty Hospital (MSH) was launched in 2010 as a flagship of the Medica North Bengal Clinic (MNBC). It is a part of the Medica Hospitals chain which is a leading chain of healthcare facilities in the Eastern region.

One of the most trusted and respected tertiary healthcare facilities in Eastern India, Medica Superspecialty has 18 departments and eight Centres of Excellence with renowned surgeons and specialists.

The hospital has a capacity of +400 beds.

It has one of the most advanced Critical Care Units in the region that is equipped with sophisticated medical infrastructure to offer life-saving care in emergency and for post-surgical patients.

The hospital has a dedicated dialysis wing with the latest haemodialysis facilities with ultrapure water and round-the-clock specialist support. The hospital also offers comprehensive diagnosis services, including a NABL-accredited lab (including a molecular lab) and imaging services that meet international standards.

Medica Superspecialty offers ACLS ambulance facility for transport of critical patients and its Medica Emergency services are available 24*7.

The hospital also offers various packages under its Home Care Services, where hospital-like care and facilities are customised for the elderly and those with limited mobility or other medical conditions.

It has a blood bank and pharmacist within its premises.

The hospital premises are modern, well-lit and spacious.
